What are the data in this dashboard?
The map on the left shows the Economic Complexity of each Local Authority District in Great Britain. The method for computing this value is described in more detail in the blog post accompanying this data visualisation.
The chart on the right shows data about the area selected on the map including: the number of businesses; the complexity of the industry; the percentage growth of that industry between 2011 and 2015; and the probability of that area growing in 2016. The chart can be zoomed in by selecting an area with your mouse; you can double click to zoom back out. Data are courtesy of the Office for National Statistics. Our methodology can be found on GitHub.
